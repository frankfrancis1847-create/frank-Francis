Admission Process

1

Application Submission

Complete and submit the online application form along with required documentation including transcripts, test scores, and recommendation letters.

2

Interview & Assessment

Schedule an interview with our admissions team and complete any required assessments appropriate for your grade level.

3

Decision Notification

Receive admission decision within two weeks of completed application. Accepted students will receive enrollment information.

4

Enrollment Confirmation

Submit enrollment forms and tuition deposit (if applicable) to secure your place in the upcoming school year.

Grade Level Requirements

Kindergarten (Age 5 by September 1)

  • Birth certificate
  • Immunization records
  • Physical examination form
  • Recent photo
  • Preschool evaluation (if available)

Grades 1-8

  • Completed application form
  • Current school transcripts
  • Standardized test scores
  • Teacher recommendations
  • Birth certificate and immunization records

Grades 9-12

  • Completed application form
  • Official transcripts from previous schools
  • Standardized test scores (SAT/ACT for 11th/12th grade)
  • Teacher and counselor recommendations
  • Personal essay
  • Extracurricular activity list

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application deadline?
Applications for the following school year are accepted beginning October 1st. Priority deadline is January 15th, with rolling admissions continuing until all spaces are filled.
Is there a tuition fee?
As a public school, [School Name] does not charge tuition. However, fees for certain programs, materials, and activities may apply.
Do you offer financial aid?
While we don't offer traditional financial aid, we work with families to ensure that no qualified student is unable to attend due to financial constraints. Payment plans are available.
What if my child has special needs?
We have robust special education and support services. Please indicate any special requirements on your application so we can discuss appropriate accommodations during the interview process.
